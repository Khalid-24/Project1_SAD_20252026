First Aid & Safety Guidelines

  • Stay calm and assess the situation before acting.
  • For severe bleeding, apply firm pressure with a clean cloth and seek help immediately.
  • For suspected fainting, lay the person flat and raise their legs slightly.
  • For breathing difficulty, keep the person upright and call the hotline immediately.
  • Never move a person with a suspected spinal injury unless absolutely necessary.
